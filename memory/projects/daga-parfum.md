## What it is

Luxury fragrance e-commerce mockup. Online store with cart drawer, product cards, sizes (30ml/100ml), Discovery Set upsell ($30 for 4 × 2ml decants), Wompi checkout button.

Founded by **Gabriel Montes** (originally Daniela Gómez — renamed at Mike's request).

## Brand identity

- **Aesthetic:** "luxury without excess" — Le Labo / Tom Ford / Maison Margiela territory
- **Palette:** bone (`#F4EFE7`), ivory (`#EBE3D5`), ink (`#0F0E0C`), brass accent (`#A88B5B`)
- **Typography:**
  - Display: **Cormorant Garamond** (editorial serif, italic for emphasis)
  - Wordmark: wide-tracked sans (Inter), all caps, 0.42em letter-spacing
  - Body: Inter
- **Logo:** "DAGA · PARFUM" with brass dot separator

## Four signature fragrances

1. **Bruma** (Mist) — fresh/floral · bergamot, white tea, jasmine, cedar — $180
2. **Selva** (Jungle) — woody/spicy · pink pepper, cardamom, vetiver, oud — $220 (featured · Extrait)
3. **Cordillera** (Mountain range) — green/earthy · juniper, rosemary, cedar, amber — $195
4. **Ópalo** (Opal) — soft/warm · pear, iris, sandalwood, vanilla — $210
