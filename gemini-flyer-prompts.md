## Tips when running these in Gemini
- Gemini sometimes mangles long Spanish text — if a flyer comes back with garbled words, regenerate or add: "Render all Spanish text exactly as written, no spelling changes."
- If text is too small to read, add: "Make the headline text large and highly legible."
- If output is too cluttered, add: "Less is more — leave generous white space."
- For variations, ask Gemini to do "3 variations of the same prompt" and pick the best.
