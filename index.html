Common questions

What people ask before they say yes.

Is the price really $500 CAD? What's the catch? +
No catch. That's the all-in price for the Essential tier. We're a Canadian-owned studio operating from Medellín; Anthropic's Claude does the design and build under our supervision. The infrastructure (Cloudflare's edge, security headers, schema markup) is the same modern stack any well-built site uses. We're not paying for an agency's overhead, so neither are you.
What if my business doesn't fit a template? +
It probably doesn't, and that's fine. We don't use templates. Every site is custom-coded around your shop: your photos, your menu or services, your hours, your booking flow, your brand. We've built sites for cafes, clinics, restaurants, retail boutiques, a fragrance e-commerce brand, an events venue, a B2B SaaS, and a finance recruiting firm. None of them look the same.
How does the 48-hour timeline actually work? +
Day 1: a 30-minute intake call (or a long WhatsApp thread, your call) where we capture your brief, photos, copy, and brand. Day 2: we build and send a staging link by the next morning. Day 2 to 3: revisions and launch on your domain. Most clients are live within 48 to 72 hours of the deposit clearing.
What if I don't like the first version? +
Free revisions for 1 week after launch on Essential, 2 weeks on Pro. Most revisions ship the same day. No round-counting, no "you've used up your edits" awkwardness. After the included period, ad-hoc edits are billed at $75 CAD/hr with a 30-min minimum. And if you're still not satisfied within 30 days of launch, our money-back guarantee covers a full refund.
Is there a money-back guarantee? +
Yes. If you're not happy with the launched site for any reason within 30 days, we take it offline and refund the full amount paid. No questions, no haggling.
How does payment work? +
30% deposit to start (we don't begin design or development until the deposit clears) and 70% on launch (we hold the finished site on staging and don't connect your domain until the balance lands). North American clients pay via Wise Business in CAD or USD: card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or wire. Latin American clients pay via Wompi in COP: card, PSE, or bank transfer.
What if I pay the deposit and then change my mind? +
If you cancel before we deliver the first preview, we refund 100% of the deposit within 14 business days, no questions. If you cancel after the first preview but before launch, the deposit is non-refundable (it covers the design work already done) and we don't invoice the 70% balance, you walk away with no further obligation. If you cancel within 30 days after launch, our money-back guarantee covers a full refund.
Do I own the site? Can I move it later? +
You own everything. Source code, copy, designs, domain. If you ever want to leave, we package the full source as a portable archive that runs on any host supporting Cloudflare Workers, Netlify, Vercel, or even a basic static server.
Can you do bilingual sites? +
Yes. Bilingual EN/FR or EN/ES is included in the Pro tier. We handle the translation, the language switch, the SEO hreflang tags, and the OG metadata for both languages.
What about ongoing updates after launch? +
Hosting and maintenance are bundled at one flat charge of $15 CAD/month after the included period (1 year on Essential, 2 on Pro). Optional monthly maintenance (content updates, security patches, minor design) is $35 CAD/month on top. Ad-hoc edits at $75/hr with a 30-min minimum.
