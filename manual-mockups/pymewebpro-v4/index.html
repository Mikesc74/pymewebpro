Before you say yes.

Is this just a one-page website?
It's one page, designed to do one job: turn visitors into customers. That's not a limit, it's the point. Every section earns its place by moving the visitor closer to a yes. If you need a 30-page site with a blog and a documentation hub, we're not the right fit. If you need the one page that closes, we are.
How is this different from Wix or Squarespace?
Wix sells you a brochure builder. We build you a salesperson. Custom-coded for your business (no template), sub-1-second load, written to convert, owned by you outright. The work is in the writing and the structure, not the drag-and-drop.
Do you guarantee leads?
No. We can't promise traffic (that's your ads, your SEO, your channels) and we can't promise conversions (that depends on traffic quality and offer fit, things we don't control). What we promise is the page itself: clear structure, fast load, working form, written to convert. The 30-day money-back guarantee covers your satisfaction with the work.
What if I already have a website?
Keep it. A marketing page is the page you point your ads at. When you run a Google Ads, Meta Ads, or LinkedIn campaign, you're paying $2 to $10 per click. Sending those clicks to a homepage with five tabs and no clear next step wastes the spend. Sending them to a marketing page built to convert is what makes the ad math work. The same logic applies to social posts, email campaigns, QR codes on a flyer, and word of mouth: it's the page you point traffic at when you want it to convert. Your existing site keeps doing its job (informing). The marketing page does the new one (selling).
Who actually builds it?
Mike and Santiago run the work. Anthropic's Claude does the design and code under our supervision. We're not hiding that, we're proud of it. It's why $500 buys what used to cost $5,000$390.000 buys what used to cost $3.900.000.
Can you write the copy or do I need to?
We write the copy. You're paying for our judgment about what the page should say and how it should be said. You'll see the draft before we build, and you can rewrite anything you want.
What happens after launch?
Both tiers: a live page on your domain plus 1 year of hosting and support. You own everything we ship: the code, the copy, the analytics setup, the domain. Pro adds the wiring into your other tools (GA4, Meta Pixel, CRM, booking calendar) so the page plays nicely with whatever stack you already run. After the included year, hosting renews on a simple flat plan.
What if I don't like it?
30-day money-back guarantee. If the page doesn't work for you, we refund every dollar. We've never had to.
