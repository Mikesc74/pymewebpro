Frequently asked questions

What business owners
ask us.

How much does it cost and can I pay in installments? +

Launch offer. One-time payment from $390,000 COP (Essential) up to $690,000 COP (Growth, includes one year of hosting). Split it into 3, 6, or more installments on your credit card; availability and the number of installments depend on your bank and are arranged directly between you and the bank at the time of payment. We start the day you pay.

How long until my site is live? +

48 hours for both plans, counted from when we receive your asset pack (logo, photos, copy). If we run late on our side, we refund 100%.

Is this a multi-page site or a single page? +

By default we build single-page sites with multiple sections (home, services, gallery, testimonials, location, contact). It's the format used today by Apple, Stripe and most modern SaaS sites: fast load, professional look, and routes the visitor straight to WhatsApp or a phone call. If you need a separate blog/news page, team, FAQ or bookings page, we'll happily add it (included in the Growth plan). Blog posts are published by us: the site is professional code, not a self-edit CMS.

Do I need to know tech to have a site? +

No. We handle all the technical work: hosting, SSL certificate, code, design, and the professional email info@yourbusiness.com. You buy the domain directly from the registrar (~$50,000-$150,000 COP/year) and it stays in your name. We recommend Namecheap for reliability and support of .com, .co and .com.co; we handle the configuration at no extra cost. When you need changes after launch, we handle them for you: included monthly on a Hosting Plan, or by the hour or package without hosting.

What if I don't have a logo, copy, or photos? +

No problem. The Growth plan includes an AI-assisted logo. We cover photos with royalty-free stock or coordinate a local shoot. We write the copy ourselves based on what you tell us about your business. You approve everything before we publish.

Does it work for my type of business? +

Yes. We work with restaurants, clinics, lawyers, real estate agencies, salons, hardware stores, consultancies, and more. The structure adapts: services and testimonials for service businesses, location and hours for local shops, products and cart for online sellers. All plans include Google Maps and a WhatsApp button.

What if I don't like it? +

30-day guarantee. If within 30 days of delivery you're not satisfied, we refund 100% of the payment, no questions asked. During the build you've included revision rounds (2 on Essential, 5 on Growth). The goal is for you to launch happy.

Are prices VAT-inclusive? +

Yes. All published prices include VAT (IVA). What you see on the site is what you pay, no surprises at checkout.

Who owns the site and the domain when it's done? +

You. The domain is registered in your name, not ours. The code lives on GitHub under your account or we hand it over in full. The photos, copy, and professional email are yours once paid. No locks, no hidden rents.

Can I migrate later to another provider? +

Anytime. We hand over the full site files, domain credentials, and all access. Any developer or agency can pick up where we left off. If you decide to leave, we help you transition for 30 days at no cost.

Does it work with DIAN and electronic invoicing? +

For each payment we issue a cuenta de cobro or factura, whichever your business needs. If you require a DIAN-compliant electronic invoice with CUFE, we coordinate it at the time of billing. For direct integration of electronic invoicing into the site (for example, e-commerce with automatic issuance), we work with your existing provider: Siigo, Alegra, ContaPyme, FacturaTech, or others.

How long does support last? +

If you're on a Hosting Plan (monthly or annual), WhatsApp support and small monthly changes are included for as long as it's active. If you buy without hosting, we include technical support for the first 30 days post-launch. After that we handle changes by the hour or via a renewed Hosting Plan.

What if I want changes after launch? +

Two options: (1) on a Hosting Plan, small monthly changes are included (copy, photos, hours, offers). (2) without hosting, we handle changes by the hour or by package, depending on what you need. For redesigns or new sections we quote with no commitment.
